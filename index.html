Our Story

A Home Designed for
Togetherness

Casa Henya was built as a space where families and friends can slow down and truly be present. From shared meals to late-night stories, every corner is designed for connection, comfort, and meaningful moments.

Nestled in the peaceful countryside, our private resort offers an escape from the busy city life — a place where time moves slower and memories are made stronger.

The Experience

A Day at Casa Henya

From sunrise to starlight, every moment is designed for connection

6:00 AM

Morning Freshness

Wake up to birdsong and fresh countryside air. Sip coffee on the patio while the garden awakens, or take a peaceful morning stroll through the grounds.

12:00 PM

Pool & Shared Meals

Cool off in the pool while the grill heats up. Gather around the dining area for a feast prepared in our full kitchen — because the best conversations happen over good food.

5:30 PM

Golden Hour Magic

As the sun dips low, the garden glows golden. Perfect for photos, quiet reflection, or simply enjoying nature's daily masterpiece with loved ones.

7:00 PM

Lights & Lasting Memories

The bonfire crackles, fairy lights twinkle, and stories flow freely. Watch an outdoor movie under the stars or simply savor the magic of doing nothing together.
